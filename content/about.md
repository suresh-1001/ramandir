## Jai Sia Ram!

**Shree Sanatan Dharam Religious & Cultural Society of California, Inc** is a non-profit organization. Our Shree Ram Mandir is located about 25 miles south of San Francisco Airport, just off the Dumbarton Bridge on the peninsula.

## Mission Statement

The purpose of our cultural society is:

- To establish and maintain our Hindu Temple built and serviced in the traditional Hindu style
- To establish and maintain a Community Center
- To promote Hindu Dharam (religion and traditions) and culture
- To promote inter-religious, social, and cultural understanding
- To support humanitarian causes both here in the US and Fiji Islands

## New Temple Construction Project {#construction}

We are working toward building a permanent, traditional Hindu temple for our growing Bay Area community. Architectural plans are in progress and your donations and contributions are making this vision a reality.
