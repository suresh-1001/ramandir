Welcome

Shree Sanatan Dharam Religious & Cultural Society

ॐ

We welcome you to Shree Ram Mandir of East Palo Alto — a Hindu temple serving the Bay Area community since 2000. Located just off the Dumbarton Bridge, about 25 miles south of San Francisco Airport, our mandir is a spiritual home for the South Bay Hindu community.

Our temple is built and maintained in the traditional Hindu style, dedicated to promoting Dharma, cultural traditions, and inter-religious understanding.
